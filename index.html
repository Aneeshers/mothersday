Dear Amma,

I was going through these photos today (23 years of them!) and I kept stopping because of what's just outside the frame. I miss home. Especially the sounds from the kitchen in the morning, my room so so clean again, and a bowl of egg curry waiting on the counter, still steaming, because you know exactly when I'll be hungry.

You never make a big deal out of any of it.

These photos are a 23-year thank-you note. I hope it's a good one!

Happy Mother's Day, Amma. - Aneesh
